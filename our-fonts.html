Texas State Web Fonts

Adobe Garamond Regular

For department and site names. Medium weight only; not intended to be modified with additional font styles.

Univers 45 Light

Primary reading font for paragraph text, captions, lists, tables, etc. Can be boldfaced with one of two weights OR italicized, but not both (see variants below).

Univers 45 Light Oblique

For general emphasis, quotes, book titles, etc. within paragraph text.

Univers 55 Roman

For subtle bold emphasis, buttons, and anywhere additional contrast is needed.

Univers 65 Bold

For heavy bold emphasis within paragraph text and elsewhere.
